# Watchopolis Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

Watchopolis is a city-building game for Wear OS developed by Jeremy Laskar.

## Data Collection

Watchopolis does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No account or registration is required
- No location data is accessed
- No contacts, calendar, or files are accessed
- No device identifiers are collected
- No usage analytics or crash reporting is sent anywhere
- No advertising networks or third-party SDKs are included

The app has no INTERNET permission and makes no network connections of any kind. All game data (saved cities) remains on your device and is never transmitted.

## Third Parties

Watchopolis does not share data with any third parties because no data is collected in the first place. The app does not include any advertising, analytics, or tracking libraries.

## Changes

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
